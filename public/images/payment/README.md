# Payment Logos

Place the official payment card logos in this directory:

- `visa.svg` or `visa.png` - Visa logo
- `mastercard.svg` or `mastercard.png` - Mastercard logo
- `amex.svg` or `amex.png` - American Express logo
- `discover.svg` or `discover.png` - Discover logo
- `paypal.svg` or `paypal.png` - PayPal logo
- `apple-pay.svg` or `apple-pay.png` - Apple Pay logo

You can download the official logos from:
- Visa: https://brand.visa.com/
- Mastercard: https://brand.mastercard.com/
- American Express: https://www.americanexpress.com/us/legal/logo-usage.html
- Discover: https://www.discover.com/company/our-company/brand-guidelines/
- PayPal: https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/logo-center
- Apple Pay: https://developer.apple.com/apple-pay/marketing/

The logos should be in SVG or PNG format, and will be automatically displayed on the product page.
